Kun Peak

The Kun Peak is a part of Nun Kun mountain massif in Ladakh, India. It is the second highest summit of the massif with elevation of 23,219 ft (7,077 metres) in the western Himalayan Range, located near the Suru valley

Moraine Lake

Moraine Lake is a stunning, glacier-fed lake in Banff National Park, Alberta, Canada, renowned for its vivid turquoise water and surrounding Valley of the Ten Peaks. Situated 14 km from Lake Louise at 1,884 meters elevation, it is accessible by shuttle, Roam Transit, or commercial tours only during the summer season.

Nanda Paal

Pithoragarh district is the easternmost district in the state of Uttarakhand. It is located in the Himalayas and has an area of 7,110 km2 (2,750 sq mi) and a population of 483,439 (as of 2011).
